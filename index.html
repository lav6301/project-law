Consumer protection law

Consumer Protection Law is a set of legal frameworks and regulations designed to safeguard the rights and interests of consumers in their interactions with businesses and service providers. It aims to ensure that consumers receive fair treatment, accurate information, and quality products or services.

Labour Law

Labour law, also known as employment law, refers to the body of legal rules and regulations that govern the relationship between employers and employees in a given jurisdiction. It encompasses a wide range of matters related to employment, from the initial hiring process to the termination of employment.
